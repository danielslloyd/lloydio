In Islamic jurisprudence, the "five rulings" are:
- "fardh" (فرض) – compulsory
- "mustahabb" (مستحب) – desirable
- "mubah" (مباح) – permissible
- "makruh" (مكروه) – discouraged
- "haram" (حرام) – prohibited

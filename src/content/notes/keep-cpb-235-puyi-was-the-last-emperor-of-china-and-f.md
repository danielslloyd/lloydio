Puyi was the last emperor of China and final Qing dynasty ruler, becoming emperor at age two in 1908 but dethroned in 1912 in the Xinhai Revolution. In 1924, he was expelled from the palace and began to court the various factions fighting for control over China, including the Japanese, who installed him as the puppet ruler of Manchukuo during World War II.

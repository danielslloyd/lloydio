Christmas is dated on Dec 25th because of a traditional belief that Jesus died on the same date that he was conceived. In the West, Good Friday was set as March 25th (equivalent to 14th day of Nisan), while in the East, the 14th day of the first spring month of the Greek calendar was chosen (equivalent to April 6th). From these dates, the West fixed Jesus's birthday as March 25th + 9 months (Dec 25th), while the East fixed it as April 6th + 9 months (Jan 6th).

The UAE's oil wealth belongs to the individual emirates, with ~96% in Abu Dhabi. Dubai has little oil and diversified into trade, tourism, and finance — oil is now under 1% of its GDP. Financial transfers between emirates (including a 2008 Dubai bailout) are informal and constitutionally discretionary.

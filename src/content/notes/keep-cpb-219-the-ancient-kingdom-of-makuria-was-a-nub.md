The ancient kingdom of Makuria was a Nubian Christian kingdom that extended from North of Thebes in Egypt to Northern Sudan. After repulsing a Muslim invasion in 651, they coexisted with Islamic neighbors until they were conquered in the 14th century by the Mamluks

The most energy efficient method to stop an orbit and crash a satellite into a larger body is to thrust "forwards" to speed up the satellite to nearly escape velocity, wait until it is far from the body, then thrust "backwards" to zero the orbital velocity. Eventually the satellite will accelerate towards and then crash into the larger body. This method requires a large Delta-V to accelerate the satellite to escape velocity - then a minimal burn to cancel out the orbital velocity when far away.

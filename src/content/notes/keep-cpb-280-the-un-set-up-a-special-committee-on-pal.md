The UN set up a special committee on Palestine (UNSCOP) that toured the region, met with Zionist representatives (Arabs boycotted) and recommended a partition plan. This resolution was supported by the Soviet Union, actively lobbied for by the USA, and quietly opposed by the UK. It passed by a narrow vote in the UN General Assembly on November 27, 1947.

Rocket engine nozzles accelerate exhaust gasses and direct them straight back so that they are parallel to the flight path of the rocket. Each nozzle's shape and size is optimized for a specific altitude - large nozzles for higher altitudes (lower pressure), small nozzles for lower altitudes (higher pressure).

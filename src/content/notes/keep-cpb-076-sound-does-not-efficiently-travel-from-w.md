title: "Sound does not efficiently travel from water to air or vice versa."

Sound does not efficiently travel from water to air or vice versa. The human inner ear is optimized for air, so underwater sounds (especially high frequencies) are muffled. When humans hear underwater, much of the sound is transmitted through their bones.

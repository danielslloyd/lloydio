Before the Seven Years' War, Britain wanted to protect the Netherlands (strategic ally) and Hanover (George II's homeland). Austria was more focused on Prussia than France, so Britain had trouble getting them to commit resources to Dutch defense, and British public opinion was far more worried about Dutch defense than Hanoverian.

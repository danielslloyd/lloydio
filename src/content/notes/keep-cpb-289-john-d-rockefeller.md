John D Rockefeller's father ("Big Bill") was a traveling snake oil salesmen and bigamist who abandoned his first family

Rockefeller set up his first refinery near Cleveland so he could play railroads off of lake/canal shipping and lower his transportation costs. After gaining a dominant position in oil refining, he maintained it through what he called "cooperation", often overpaying to buy out his rivals. Those who took payment in standard oil stock often went on to become fabulously wealthy as a result.

Edith Rockefeller went to Switzerland and became a benefactor and devotee of Carl Jung and Jungian analysis
